Noella Wang - Beyond Horizons

Project Details:

Date: May 2025

Role: Composer, Producer, Sound Designer

Beyond Horizons is a 4-minute, 45-second electronic composition created entirely with modular synthesis, using the Buchla 200e, the Make Noise Shared System, and additional effect processing. The piece explores themes of exploration and the unknown, using evolving timbres, shifting textures, and dynamic contrasts to evoke a sense of traveling into uncharted sonic territory.

The piece blends elements of electronica, ambient, jazz, and sound-object composition, expanding its modular textures into a broader musical landscape.

As the sole artist, I handled all aspects of composition, sound design, and production, working through hands-on patching and real-time control. The piece reflects my interest in treating modular instruments as exploratory systems, where gesture, circuitry, and sound continually influence one another to shape the listening experience.
