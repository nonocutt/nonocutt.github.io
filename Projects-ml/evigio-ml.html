Noella Wang - Evigilatio Aeternitatis

Project Details:

Date: August 2025

Role: Composer, Producer, Sound Designer

Titled from the Latin for “Awakening of Eternity,” Evigilatio Aeternitatis is a 6-minute, 28-second electronic composition that explores ideas of vigilance, memory, and time stretching outward. The piece weaves granular textures, layered harmonies, and evolving rhythmic figures into an immersive soundscape that gradually shifts rather than moving through a traditional structure.

As the composer, producer, and sound designer, I created every aspect of the work from timbre to harmony to structure, using a combination of synthesis, sampling, and digital audio processing in Ableton Live. The piece reflects my interest in storytelling through sound alone: using gradual transformations of texture and space to suggest narrative and emotional change.
