Evigilatio Aeternitatis

Date: August 2025

Style: Breakcore, Experimental

Titled from the Latin for “Awakening of Eternity,” Evigilatio Aeternitatis is a 6-minute, 28-second electronic composition that explores ideas of vigilance, memory, and time stretching outward. The piece weaves granular textures, layered harmonies, and evolving rhythmic figures into an immersive soundscape that gradually shifts rather than moving through a traditional structure.

Beyond Horizons

Date: May 2025

Style: Experimental

Beyond Horizons is a 4-minute, 45-second electronic composition created entirely with modular synthesis, using the Buchla 200e, the Make Noise Shared System, and additional effect processing. It combines ambient, jazz, sound object composition, and synthesis techniques into a cohesive theme.

Angelic Ascension

Date: August 2024

Style: Drum and Bass

Angelic Ascension is a 4-minute electronic composition developed with a combination of virtual instruments and hardware synthesizers. It explores themes of transcendence and spiritual elevation. It highlights the shift of mood and energy through dynamic layering and textural evolution.

Tunneling Transcendence

Date: December 2023

Style: Techno

Tunneling Transcendence is a 4-minute, 5-second techno composition produced in Ableton Live. It expands upon traditional techno elements with atmospheric textures and evolving musical development for a unique listening experience.

Sensitive Margin

Date: January 2024

Style: Drum and Bass

Sensitive Margin is a 2-minute, 55-second electronic composition produced in Ableton Live.

Tales of Aster

Date: March 2024

Style: Soundtrack

Tales of Aster is a 2-minute, 10-second electronic composition in the style of early 60s synthesizer music. Inspired by Plantasia by Mort Garson, it features lush, melodic synth lines and rhythmic patterns that evoke a sense of wonder and exploration.
